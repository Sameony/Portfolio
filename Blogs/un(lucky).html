There goes a saying that "You'll be struck by immense misfortune and unluck if a black cat crosses your way" or something along those lines. What is the fault of such cute cats to be cursed and called spawns of misfortunes? why must they be blamed? There are many sayings relating to luck and unluck, and apparently, some people still believe them. Lets look into what luck is and brief through the life of world's most lucky (maybe) man.

Luck

Luck can have a lot of meanings but they all mainly point towards good things, happenings, positivity and success. Wishing luck is basically wishing for success. On the other hand, we have the term, unlucky, which, as it sounds, is the polar opposite of lucky. We have seen all kinds of people, some very lucky, winning everywhere, midas touch and stuff...and also the unlucky people, struggling through misfortunes everyday as if it is normal. What if i told you, there exists a glitch in the matrix where luck and unluck stick together?

The world's most Unlucky lucky man.

Here lies lives Mr.Frane Selak from croatia who is often dubbed as, sometimes, World's unluckiest man, sometimes as world's luckiest man, and sometimes both. Why? What in the world happened here? To understand that, lets take a look at the lifetime achievements of him.

Year 1962

Frane was travelling in a train on a chilly day in January, when the train suddenly crashed and fell into an icy river, killing 17 passengers. Our Hero survived with only a broken arm and hypothermia.

Year 1963

Just one year later, Due to an emergency, Frane had booked a flight trip to see his ill mother. Suddenly, both of the engines of the plane stopped working, and the plane crashed. 19 people on board were killed. However, soon after the engines stopped, frane was sucked out of the plane from a broken door and he crash landed on a haystack. He only suffered minor injuries. That's some serious luck right there.

Year 1966

Frane was riding a bus that skidded and crashed off the road into an icy river (again!). 4 passengers drowned, but frane swam to the shore with only minor cuts n bruises.

Year 1970

At this point, it was clear that public transport was not his cup of tea. However, it's not just public transport that's unlucky for him. In 1970, His car engine suddenly burst into flames while he was driving and luckily, he managed to jump out moments before the fuel tank exploded, resulting in him surviving through the day yet again!

year 1973

In Another episode of car driving accidents, we bring to you, Mr. Frane Selak! On a normal peaceful day, his car's fuel pump broke and suddenly, the air vents started blowing the engine's flame directly on his face! He lost some hairs, but lived through !

year 1995

He was casually taking a walk when he was suddenly hit by a bus! Guess what happened? Yeah, nothing. He survived with a few minor cuts.

Year 1996

While driving through the mountains, He had to dodge an oncoming truck and he ended up swerving into a guard rail. The rail broke and somehow, he jumped out of the car and grabbed on to a tree branch, while the car plummeted down the mountains. Obviously, at this point, it's clear that The God of Transport and Vehicles hate him.

Year 2003

Finally, in year 2003, in a surprisingly normal event, he won a Million dollar lottery! Yes, at this point, it merely looks as if the god of Transport and Vehicles were just reimbursing him his insurance money.
And that was the Luck- err, Unluck-... oh well, that was the life of Frane seluk, The world's most (un)lucky person!
